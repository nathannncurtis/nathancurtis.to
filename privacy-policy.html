Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Service Description

Morning Briefing is a personal automated SMS service that sends daily news summaries and commute information. This service is operated by and sends messages exclusively to the account owner. It is not a public-facing service and does not accept third-party subscribers.

Data Collection

This service does not collect, store, or process personal data from any third party. The only phone number used is the account owner's own number, configured directly in the application.

Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party. Message content is generated and delivered solely for the account owner's personal use.

Message Content

Messages contain automated summaries of news headlines and commute/traffic information relevant to the account owner's daily routine.

Contact
