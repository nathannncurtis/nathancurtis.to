Program Name

Morning Briefing

Description

Morning Briefing is an automated SMS service that delivers daily news summaries and commute information to the account owner. This is a personal service operated by and for a single user. It does not accept external subscribers.

Message Frequency

Up to 5 messages per week, delivered on weekday mornings.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

Opt-Out

Text STOP to the sending number at any time to stop receiving messages.

Help

Text HELP to the sending number for support, or contact nathan@nathancurtis.to.
